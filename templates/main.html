Methods

The serving model is trained on pre-trained image xlm-roberta.

Limitations

Each data point fed to the model during inference is a pair of (q, t), in which q is a textual question, t is the transcript of a Youtube vlog. The goal of the model is to extract the answer a, a word span in the reference transcript t. The main challenge is the comprehensibility of the transcript:

  • The selected vlogs are genuinely created by humans of the Vietnamese com- munity and they may speak different Vietnamese dialects. Vietnamese is also a language with tones, one word may be pronounced differently among speakers. The transcripts were generated primarily by YouTube; therefore, they contain errors due to automatic speech recognition (ASR).
  • The platform’s ASR system hardly uses any form of punctuation, including the period ”.” and the comma ”,”. As a result, the transcript is a lengthy collection of speeches, which could be monologues or dialogues, and are not separated appropriately.
